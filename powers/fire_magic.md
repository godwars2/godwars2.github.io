title: Fire Magic
description: Improved fire magic.
info:
  Required class      : Mage
  Power sphere        : Magic
reqs:
  Requirements        : None
---

This power grants you access to a range of fire spells.  Each rank gives you
+10 range, +2 Ascendancy and +1 Power to each fire spell, and also increases
the duration and stat bonuses of fire spell affects.

A single rank in this power will give you the Firebolt and Summon Elemental
spells.  At rank 2 you learn Fever.  At rank 3 you learn Fireball.  At rank 4
you learn Immolate and Fireblade.  At rank 5 you learn Firestorm.  At rank 6
you learn Burning Fury.  At rank 8 you learn Resist Energy (heat).  At rank 10
you learn Fireshield and a second Firebolt.
