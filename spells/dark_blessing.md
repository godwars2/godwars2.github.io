    Spell type          : Defensive buff (blue spell)
    Requires            : Dark Lineage or Witch Spawn
    Enhanced by         : Path of Shadows and Pathway to Hell
    Casting time        : 3 seconds
    Base modifiers      : 25 feet range
    Duration            : 60 seconds (modified by green magic)
    Enhanced mana cost  : 25 mana makes the duration permanent
---

Dark Blessing is a defensive buff which gives the recipient +10 Attack and 
Damage, +1 per rank in Path of Shadows or Pathway to Hell.  The Attack bonus 
is also modified by blue magic.
